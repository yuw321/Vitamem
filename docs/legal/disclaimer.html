Legal & Safety Disclaimer

Not a Medical Device

Vitamem is a software developer library for conversation memory management. It is not a medical device, clinical decision support system, or healthcare product.

Vitamem is not intended to: - Diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition - Replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment - Store or process Protected Health Information (PHI) as a covered entity under HIPAA

Developer Responsibility

If you are building a health or wellness application using Vitamem, you (the developer) are responsible for:

  • Ensuring your application complies with applicable laws and regulations, including HIPAA, GDPR, CCPA, and any other relevant privacy laws
  • Implementing appropriate security measures, including encryption at rest and in transit
  • Establishing Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with your storage providers if handling PHI
  • Including your own privacy policy and terms of service
  • Adding appropriate safety disclaimers in your application's user interface
  • Directing users to seek professional medical advice for clinical decisions

Recommended Safety Notice for Applications

Include a notice like the following in any application you build with Vitamem:

This AI companion is not a licensed healthcare provider. The information and support provided here are for informational and wellness purposes only. For medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, always consult a qualified healthcare professional. In case of a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately.

Mental Health Applications

Applications providing mental health support should additionally:

  • Display crisis hotline information prominently (e.g., 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in the US)
  • Not position the AI as a replacement for therapy or psychiatric care
  • Be transparent with users about the AI's limitations
